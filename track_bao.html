Introduction

The master of Sciences in Bioinformatics of the University of Bordeaux and the “Titulo propio” Bioinformatics for High Throughput Sequencing of the University of Basque Country UPV/EHU propose an International Master Program entitled "Bioinformatics And Omics" dealing with the combined training of Metagenomics and Bioinformatics. This international program is both based on the experience of metagenomics data of the UPV/EHU, and the experience in training bioinformatics to biologists of the MSc in Bioinformatics of Bordeaux University.

The track BAO is a two-year post-graduate Master program, entirely taught in English in the first year, and designed to the new challenges of analyzing complex “Omics” data at the interface of Bioinformatics and MetaGenomics.

The international specialization of the master of “Bioinformatics and Omics“ is a two-full-year program. It will provide a combined training for students from UBx and from UPV/EHU with a mobility in Bordeaux for the first semester and Bilbao for the second semester.

Application

Titulo Proprio, Bilbao: One-year Formation

If you already have a master degree (according to Bologne process) in Biology and are looking for a one-year specialization in Bioinformatics, the Titulo Proprio — equivalent to the french Diplôme Universitaire — could be a good option compared to the 2-year master degree in Bordeaux. All the details about the Titulo Proprio — supervised by Dr. A. Estonba Rekalde and Dr. I. Zarraonaindia Martinez — are available in this web page and in the official UPV/EHU university web page.

Note: The Titulo Proprio is only available to students validating a 4-year bachelor degree in Spain or the first year of master degree elsewhere in Europe).

MSc in Bioinformatics, Bordeaux: Two-year Formation

If you have a bachelor degree (according to Bologne process) in Biology, you may apply for the Master of Sciences in Bioinformatics of University of Bordeaux. The procedure — depending of your nationality, diploma,etc. — may be different and is detailed in the homepage of this website.

Financial Aid for accommodation

All the students may qualify for student accommodation aid from the CAF (Caisse d'Allocations Familiales — Family Benefits Agency). You may complete — in french — your application on­line via www.caf.fr

For this aid, you must:
  • be a student
  • have a rental contract in your name
  • not be responsible for any children.
For non­-EU student, you will need a residence permit valid for over 3 months.

Security Deposit/Rental payment guarantee

When renting an apartment, in most cases the payment of a security deposit is required (1 month rent) as well as rental payment guarantee (this means providing the contact details of someone who will pay the rent if you are not able to).

Accommodation Insurance

Personal insurance is obligatory no matter your accommodation, whether you are staying with a friend or renting in the private sector.

Spring Semester — 30 ECTS

The spring semester of the second year (semester 10) is dedicated to an internship that has to cover a period range of 6 months in a private company or in an academic laboratory abroad. Over the years, the number of oversea internships have increased due to the appreciation of our students skills.
Also, the development of a network of former students (leading a position today) provides to our current students a wide range of internship opportunities in different fields of applications.

Le second semestre de la seconde année (semestre 10) est dédié à un stage d'une durée de 6 mois. Au fil des années, le nombre de stages réalisés à l'étranger n'a cessé d'augmenter grâce à la reconnaissance des compétences de nos étudiants.
